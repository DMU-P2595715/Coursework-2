The big Push is a four man band based in Brighton. The band consists of three frontment Ren, Romain Axisa, Gorran Kendall and the drummer Glen Chambers.

Drawing heavy influence from classic British bands such as The Beatles and The Police, The Big Push combine many music techniques and modern arrangements to create their own unique sounds which many fans have come to love.

The Big Push started their career busking in Brighton with a crazed, manic energy that creates a compeltely authentic Rock and Roll sound. They quickly blew up and went from busking on Brightons bustling streets to headlining and playing at the Isliongton O2 Academy at full capacity.

Over the last year the band have gained a large international following amassing over 1.2 million streams on Spotify as well as 12 million views on Youtube. Having achieved such staggering success online, thier fanbase now reaches across the globe.
